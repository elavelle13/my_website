layover mobile application

Layover is a mobile application concept I created for a potential internship opportunity at Zappos. I was required to create a mobile application that dealt with a user's needs in an airport when they had connecting flights, delays, and so on. I conducted research on color theories, users' needs, and airport layouts to fully get a grasp on what was needed and not needed. I created low-fidelity mockups, reviewed all layouts, and then created a high-fidelity mockup.

Out of the hundreds of applications they received, I was one of the 4 chosen to meet the company at their headquaters in Las Vegas, NV for the UX Designer Internship program.
