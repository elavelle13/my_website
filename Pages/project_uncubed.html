Uncubed web design & email templates

I was employed at NYC Uncubed from May - August 2016. I lived in the city in Union Square, and worked in China Town. Through this experience, I learned to become a better web designer, understand UX Design better, and learn how a small business works.

As the Tech Intern at Uncubed, I was responsible for creating various web pages - amongst other tasks. I worked on two major sections: For Companies and Delivery. I worked with the Tech Lead through GitHub to collaborate on pages. In addition, I helped maintain the current site and minor design changes.

Another task I was responsible for was the creation of their email templates for their daily newsletter. This daily newsletter is sent out to hundreds of thousands of readers all over the world. The two emails I created from scratch are still in use today.

Please Note: Pages on the site today might have changed within the past couple of years.
