division of student affairs web design

The Division of Student Affairs at Kent State University is home to the Vice President's office. Four designers and myself make up the department's design team, also known as DSA Design. My role within the department is the User Interface Design Specialist & Design Lead. I am responsible for hiring and managing the design team members, maintaining and developing the website, and overseeing all of the design projects in our office. I ensure the brand standards of the Unviersity are met and followed at all times, but also allow room for creativity and uniqueness.
